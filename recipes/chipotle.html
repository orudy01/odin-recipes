Homemade Chipotle

Enjoy this delicious chicken bowl while saving money!

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds of boneless chicken thighs
  • 3 roma tomatoes
  • 8 avocados
  • 1 can of chipotle peppers
  • 2 1/2 cups of white rice
  • 1 large can of black beans

Instructions

  1. Blend the chipotle peppers and place in a bag with the chicken
  2. Make guacomole
  3. Make pico de gallo
  4. Make white rice with canola oil and bay leaves
  5. Grill chicken and season with salt
  6. Make black beans
